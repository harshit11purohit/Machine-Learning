📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Playstore App Analysis (PLAYSTORE.IPYNB): Comprehensive analysis of app ratings, categories, and installations to identify market trends.

Travel Industry Insights (eda_travel.ipynb & filghtravel.ipynb): Data visualization of travel booking patterns and flight price determinants.

Student Performance (student_performance.ipynb): Identifying key factors (socio-economic and academic) that influence student grades.

🧠 Classification & Predictive Modeling
Chronic Disease Prediction (chronicdisease.ipynb): A healthcare-focused model to predict the likelihood of chronic illness based on patient vitals.

HR Analytics (HR_Analytics.ipynb): Predicting employee attrition and identifying factors behind staff turnover.

USA Visa Approval (usa_visa.ipynb): Analyzing visa applications to predict the probability of approval based on applicant profiles.
