### 1. Air Passengers Forecasting (`air_passengers.ipynb`)
* **Dataset:** `AirPassengers.csv`
* **Objective:** Predict future airline passenger traffic.
* **Key Concepts:** * Handling **Multiplicative Seasonality** (variation grows with the trend).
    * **Stationarity** testing using the Augmented Dickey-Fuller (ADF) test.
    * Time Series Decomposition (Trend, Seasonality, and Noise).
    * **SARIMA** modeling for seasonal data.

### 2. Tesla Stock Price Analysis (`TSLA_Time_series.ipynb`)
* **Dataset:** `TSLA.csv`
* **Objective:** Analyze volatility and price movement of Tesla (TSLA) stock.
* **Key Concepts:**
    * Working with **Non-Stationary** financial data.
    * Transforming prices into **Log Returns** to stabilize variance.
    * Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of stock market trends and "Random Walks."

### 3. Time Series Fundamentals (`Time_Series_Forecasting.ipynb`)
* **Objective:** A comprehensive guide to time series components and forecasting workflows.
* **Contents:** * Visualizing **Level, Trend, and Seasonality**.
    * Understanding **Smoothing** techniques (Moving Averages, Exponential Smoothing).
    * Detailed look at **ACF** and **PACF** plots for model selection.
    * The difference between **AR, MA, ARIMA, and SARIMA** models.
